Our Mission

We seek formal recognition of cognitive liberty as a fundamental human right — the right to mental privacy, freedom from manipulation, and self-determination over one’s own mind.

Modern Threats to Thought

  • AI Persuasion: Subtle cognitive manipulation via algorithms.
  • Neuro-Intrusion: Non-consensual decoding of brain activity.
  • Brain Data Exploitation: Commodification of mental signals.
  • Deepfakes & Disinfo: Undermining truth and trust.

What We’re Doing

Policy Advocacy

We engage with lawmakers to push for legal frameworks that protect mental autonomy.

Education Campaigns

We inform the public on the threats and rights surrounding cognitive liberty.

Ethical Innovation

We promote responsible tech aligned with human rights and dignity.
